The Unseen Realms

Exploring the supernatural worldview, the hidden spiritual dimensions, and the divine beings that operate beyond human perception.

Overview

"The Unseen Realm" refers to a book by Dr. Michael Heiser that explores the supernatural worldview of the Bible, focusing on the ancient context of divine beings like angels and demons and their role in biblical history. The book argues that understanding this spiritual reality is crucial for a complete understanding of Scripture. It translates complex scholarly concepts into accessible language for readers interested in biblical studies.

"The Bible is an ancient book, and understanding its worldview on its own terms is essential to understanding its message."

Key Themes

  • Spiritual Battles: The book presents the Christian life as involving a spiritual battle, with the unseen spiritual realm influencing the physical world.
  • Divine Council: Heiser discusses the concept of a divine council — a gathering of spirit beings under God's rule — and its implications for understanding passages that mention "gods" or other divine beings.
  • Supernatural Worldview: The book aims to help readers see the Bible through the eyes of its ancient authors, who fully embraced a supernatural worldview, rather than through modern theological filters.
