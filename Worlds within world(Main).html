Unraveling the Dimensional Fabric

Kaluza-Klein Theory

A classic attempt to unify gravitation with electromagnetism by introducing a **fifth spatial dimension**. This dimension is theorized to be 'compactified' or rolled up, making it too small to be observed directly, yet its influence shapes the forces we experience.

M-Theory & The 11 Dimensions

Modern **String Theory** (specifically M-Theory) suggests the universe exists in **11 dimensions**: our 4 spacetime dimensions plus 7 additional, curled-up dimensions. These extra dimensions are necessary to make the mathematical framework consistent and may hold the key to a unified 'Theory of Everything'.
